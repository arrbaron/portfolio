Projects I Made

Out of code and magic (but mostly code)

Hangmanateach

Hangmanateach is a vocab studying tool for both learners and educators. Create your own study sets, test spelling in a fun and familiar format, and get quizzed on meaning. Also, manatees!

Fridge Poetry

Fridge Poetry lets users build poems together with strangers on the internet. With magnets!

Weather to Game

Weather to Game pulls from two separate APIs to let you know whether the weather is right for playing a board game. Afterwards, it's even kind enough to suggest a game for you to play!
